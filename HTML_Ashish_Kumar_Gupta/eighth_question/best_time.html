Best Time to Visit Jammu and Kashmir

What is the best time to visit Jammu and Kashmir?

J&K is a four-season travel destination, you can go all year round basis your preference of season and it will never disappoint you. Having said that, October- March is considered a popular time in the local tourism industry. The weather during this time is most ideal and suitable for sightseeing.

Monthly Weather in Srinagar

Srinagar has pleasant weather with a soothing temperature making it a go-to destination throughout the year. However, April to October is the best time to visit Srinagar. With freezing winters and hot summers, Srinagar is best visited during the Autumn months from September to November however, monsoons are also a great time that transforms this beautiful place into a magnificent landscape.

Monthly Weather in Gulmarg

The best time to visit Gulmarg is in Summer from March - June. The hill station is often visited for the adventurous opportunities that await visitors and the scenic views of the surroundings from the town. If you are a nature enthusiast who loves the heat, the months April, May and June are the best months for a visit. Gulmarg is also known as the 'meadow of flowers' due to the fields of blooming flowers that cover the lands of the city in Summer. However, If skiing is what you are looking forward to, December to mid March are recommended.

Monthly Weather in Vaishno Devi

Vaishno Devi Yatra is open throughout the year for pilgrims and tourists. However, the best time to visit Vaishno Devi is from March to October. The weather is comfortable during these months, and nature is at its best during the monsoon.

The original Holy Cave of Vaishno Devi is open during the winters and is mystical to experience. However, from November to February, Vaishno Devi witnesses sub-zero temperature. So, until you're physically fit and a snow enthusiast, winter season is not recommended for a visit.

The festivals of Sharad (Autumn), the period of Chaitra (Spring) and the festival of Navratras and New Year witness peak rush. Visit during this time only if you do not mind waiting 15-20 hours for the darshan.
