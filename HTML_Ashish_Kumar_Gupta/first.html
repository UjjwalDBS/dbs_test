Header tag is used to show title and heading of related content.

Header tag is usally contains heading tags.

Header tag con't be used under footer,address or another header tag.

There are 6 heading tags.

h1 h2 h3 h4 5 h6 are 6 heading tags.
h1 h2 h3 h4 h5 h6 are in the order of their decreasing size.
