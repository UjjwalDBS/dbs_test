Things To Do In Ladakh

Experience Ladakh like never before and get ready to be mesmerized by the striking landscapes. Things to do in Ladakh can range from a camel safari to trying the Yak cheese momos. It is a perfect destination for back-packers and adventure lovers. If you are ready to travel alone, you must rent a bike and head to Ladakh. Most of the backpackers rent a bike from Manali and head to Ladakh. It is the most satisfying journey because you get to meet new people and witness the awesome views. What are the best things to do in Leh Ladakh? If you are looking for adventure sports in Leh Ladakh, you will be happy to know that there are activities like river rafting, mountain biking, trekking, and jeep safari. You will enjoy it thoroughly!

    Nature Walk In Ladakh

    Ladakh is a heavenly place, full of picturesque beauty. The astounding views of majestic mountain ranges, spectacular lakes, blissful monasteries, rare beautiful species, lush green fields, unspoiled villages make it a popular tourist destination in India. To have a mind blowing adventure, Leh in Ladakh region is the best place to travel. Though many places of Ladakh don’t have proper communication, it still makes us communicate with nature. Being disconnected with the outside world is the best part of this place. Get connected with the tremendous beauty of Ladakh with nature walks. Going on nature walks in Ladakh not only exercises our body but also soothes our mind.

    Camping Adventure, Ladakh

    Camping is an adventurous activity preferred by travelers who love the great outdoors and spending time surrounded by the beauty of nature. Getting away from the luxury of our home to enjoy a peaceful night under the star lit sky is a different experience. Resting in such a surrounding relaxes our mind and body. Being in that serene environment is equal to meditation which enables self-awareness. Camping allows enjoying nature along with knowing yourself. Camping in Ladakh is very popular among adventure seekers. Accommodation in most of the tourist areas of Ladakh is either unavailable or costly. Hence, camping is the best option which is both cost effective and thrilling. Mobile signals are not available in most of the tourist areas of Ladakh. Spending your days in camping in Leh Ladakh without mobiles, traffic, climatic heat and the pressure of daily life is a tremendous experience beyond words. There are many camping sites available in the lively land of Ladakh. For cheaper accommodation, choose Chadar tents. The prices of other camping sites vary according to the comfort and location of the camp.

    Trekking In Ladakh

    Ladakh is known for its unending lively places to enjoy. Walking through the hot spots of Ladakh and enjoying the scenic views of this lovely land is a blissful experience. Trekking involves walking around the less traveled places of the world irrespective of how hard the surroundings are. However, this is the adventurous aspect of trekking, which attracts many people to go on trekking. Trekking is beneficial to both physical and spiritual wellness. When walking has these many benefits, why don’t we try trekking? Enjoying the serene beautiful views while walking on the virgin natural path is a delightful experience. The sense of accomplishing something at the end of trekking is the highlight of this activity. Trekking in Ladakh region provides a unique trekking experience.

    White Water Rafting In Ladakh

    White Water Rafting is an exciting water sport in which a raft is inflated and used to navigate on a river. This sport is adventurous and risky which can give an adrenaline rush throughout the activity. There are various destinations in India which offers White Water Rafting. Ladakh is one of the most popular rafting spots in India. Adventure seekers flock to Ladakh to enjoy this attractive river rafting in Leh Ladakh. White Water Rafting in Ladakh provides an astounding view of snow clad mountain peaks, serene monasteries, hillside pleasant villages. Rafting in Ladakh happens from one day to a week expedition.
